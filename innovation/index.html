More about P-AST™ Evidence
Our Approach

Instead of relying on resistance gene information alone, Guidance® UTI leverages Pooled Antibiotic Susceptibility Testing (P-AST™) — a unique technology that accounts for bacterial interactions in polymicrobial infections that may alter antibiotic resistance.1

Tests for mutualism. Faster turnaround. Personalized results.

Guidance® UTI leverages unique technology to transform patient care and champion antibiotic stewardship by better addressing polymicrobial infections, where bacterial interactions can impact antibiotic resistance. Guidance UTI helps providers direct personalized treatment to resolve infections in complicated UTIs.

While culture can take 3–5 days, Guidance UTI provides personalized results, including antibiotic susceptibility, in less than one day from receipt at lab‡ — reducing the need for empiric therapy and supporting antibiotic stewardship initiatives.

Data Repository & Biobanking

A robust research resource that facilitates the exploration of biological determinants of health and disease.

Our large data repository includes over 300,000 cases of suspected UTI data. We compare positive vs. negative cases, polymicrobial cases, antibiotic susceptibility, and regional, seasonal, and demographic differences.
