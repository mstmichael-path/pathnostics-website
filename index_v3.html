A Fundamentally Different Diagnostic Category

Detection Isn't Direction.

Total Infection Response Testing evaluates antibiotic effectiveness at the level of the complete infection — assessing therapeutic response in the conditions that actually exist in the patient. This isn't a better version of the same test. It's a different clinical question entirely.
